Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.

    Best Places to Dine:

  1. Gentle Brook Cafe
  2. The Shack
  3. Subway
  4. Mc Donald's
  5. Chung Gi Wa

Gentle Brook Cafe | Susupe, Saipan

Gentle Brook Cafe is my to-go-to whenever me and my family don't have any idea on where to eat out! I love getting their Tonkatsu Noodles there and their portions are also really good! I recommend it wholeheartedly.

    What I Love:

  • Their menu is honestly really great!
  • They dress up on halloween!
  • Lovely indoor aesthetic
